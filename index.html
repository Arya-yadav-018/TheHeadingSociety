Different ways to heal yourself

Sound Healing

Sound Healing is a powerful therapy that combines different healing sounds, music, and sound healing instruments to improve our multidimensional well-being by creating a beautiful experience where all layers of our luminous energy field are awakened gently and lovingly.

Yoga

Yoga is used as a therapy for cancer, infertility, lung disease, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’s disease, insomnia, high blood pressure, and joint pain. Yet there is very little awareness and understanding on exactly how yoga heals, even in the yoga and medical communities.

Pranic healing

Pranic Healing is an energy “no-touch” healing system based on the fundamental principle that the body has the innate ability to heal itself. Pranic Healing utilizes “life force,” “energy,” or prana to accelerate the body’s inborn ability to heal itself.

Acupuncture Healing

Acupuncture points are believed to stimulate the central nervous system. This, in turn, releases chemicals into the muscles, spinal cord, and brain. These biochemical changes may stimulate the body's natural healing abilities and promote physical and emotional well-being.

What We Provide

Communication

Therapy can help you learn how to have healthy, productive, positive communication skills. Being able to effectively communicate in a healthy way means you can successfully navigate virtually all areas of your life. Whether you tend to shy away from confrontation, get angry too quickly, or feel anxiety when having a direct conversation, therapy can help you gain the skills you need to feel confident any time you’re communicating with others.

Mental Health

Therapy can be effective in treating a number of mental health conditions, including anxiety and depression, obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), and more. Putting your mental health first and getting the help you need is a sign of strength and something to be proud of.

Physical Health

There is a strong connection between mental and physical health, and therapy can help improve both. For example, therapy can help you learn how to manage stress, which can lead to improved sleep, reduced blood pressure, and a stronger immune system.
